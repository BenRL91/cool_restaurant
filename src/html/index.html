Our Location

The idea for the Noma concept was born when three friends, all from different walks of life, decided to create a restaurant to rival all others.

Britt, the artist of the group, designed a restaurant where diners would feel as if they have been transformed into a beautiful dream, where the colors are more vivid, the sights are more clear and the space feels magical.





Ben, the engineer, made sure that all of Britts designs were beautiful and functional at the same time. He made sure every table, every chair and every stove was installed exactly where it should be and that all parts were in working order.

Mallerie, the chef, brought her heart and soul into the most important part of the operation, the cuisine. She brought innovative dishes that the world will crave.

Our dream is that our restaurant is the place where stories are born. This is a place we hope you'll want to tell your grandchildren about.
